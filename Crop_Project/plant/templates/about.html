🚨 Problem Statement

Agriculture remains the backbone of many economies, yet farmers continue to face significant challenges in identifying plant diseases at early stages. Traditional methods rely heavily on manual inspection, which is time-consuming, requires expert knowledge, and is often inaccurate.

Due to delayed diagnosis, farmers suffer from reduced crop yield, increased pesticide usage, and financial losses. In rural areas, access to agricultural experts is limited, making the problem even more critical.

  • ❌ Lack of early disease detection
  • ❌ Dependence on agricultural experts
  • ❌ Increased crop damage and loss
  • ❌ Limited access to proper guidance

💡 Proposed Solution

Krushi Mitra is an AI-powered crop health diagnostic system designed to provide instant, accurate, and accessible plant disease detection using deep learning.

By simply uploading an image of a plant leaf, farmers can receive real-time analysis, disease identification, causes, and treatment recommendations without requiring expert intervention.

  • ✅ AI-based disease prediction using CNN models
  • ✅ Instant diagnosis with high accuracy
  • ✅ Smart fertilizer & treatment recommendations
  • ✅ Multi-language support for better accessibility
